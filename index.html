Our Products

At Umeys we offer a wide selection of high-quality sinks to fit every style, space, and budget.

Adjustable Shower Poles
Stainless steel and chrome-finish shower poles, adjustable for various heights and compatible with most showerheads and curtains. Resistant to corrosion and daily wear, these poles feature universal mounting brackets and extendable lengths to fit a variety of bathroom layouts.
Hand Wash Sink
Compact and space-saving, perfect for small bathrooms or commercial washrooms. Easy to maintain and built with hygienic materials. Their minimalist design integrates well with modern interiors, while optional backsplash panels and integrated soap trays enhance functionality in high-traffic areas.
Toilet Seat
Ergonomic, durable toilet seats with soft-close hinges to prevent slamming. Available in standard and slim designs. Constructed from anti-bacterial polypropylene or duroplast materials, they are easy to clean and install, with quick-release buttons for hassle-free maintenance.
Stainless Steel Kitchen Sink
Crafted from high-grade stainless steel for rust resistance, noise reduction, and modern aesthetics—ideal for any kitchen setup. These sinks are designed with deep bowls and anti-condensation coatings, making them perfect for heavy-duty cleaning while maintaining a clean, quiet kitchen environment.
Ceramic
Fired at high temperatures, Ceramic features a non-porous, glossy surface that resists scratches, stains, and moisture. Crafted from high-quality glazed ceramic, this product offers a sleek, durable, and easy-to-clean surface for any bathroom.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our sink products, services, and support.

What types of sinks do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of kitchen, bathroom, and commercial sinks including stainless steel, ceramic, under-mount, top-mount, and more to suit every space and design aesthetic.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services through our certified partners across all major cities. You can schedule installation during checkout or contact our support team after purchase.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. For custom or bulk orders, shipping may take slightly longer. Tracking information is provided once your order ships.

Do your sinks come with a warranty?

All our sinks come with a manufacturer’s warranty of 5 to 10 years, depending on the model. Our products are quality checked and backed by our satisfaction guarantee.

Can I return or exchange a sink after purchase?

Yes, we offer a hassle-free 14-day return or exchange policy as long as the product is unused and in original packaging. Please check our return policy page or contact our support team.

Do you offer discounts on bulk or commercial orders?

We offer special pricing and discounts for contractors, interior designers, and businesses on bulk or repeat orders. Please reach out to our B2B support team for personalized quotes.
